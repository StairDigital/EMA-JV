About

An institution built for the board.

Board work is mandated for thousands of companies, yet it remains largely unserved by any dedicated, independent institution. EMAS exists to close 3 gaps that personal networks cannot.

01
The composition gap

Search, not courtesy

Seats are filled through personal address books rather than structured, independent search, and the annual evaluation every listed company must run is too often a compliance file, not a source of insight.

02
The capability gap

Both halves, in one place

Search firms treat board seats as an extension of CXO hiring. Governance consultants advise on process but hold no director talent. Neither party holds both the discipline and the network.

03
The ownership gap

India owned, board led

Global brands carry parent reporting lines and sanction sensitivity that governance sensitive boards find uncomfortable. An India owned platform turns that discomfort into a durable advantage.

Led by people who have sat on boards, not merely filled them.

How we work

Method where the decision is still made by habit.

01

Search led, not nomination led

We advocate that board composition be a disciplined, independent search rather than a personal favour, while respecting the discretion the network has always provided.

02

A pipeline already held

From a standing pool of several hundred sitting and aspiring directors, we can say within hours which 2 or 3 names fit a seat.

03

Evaluation that is acted upon

Our framework is built to produce insight, not uniform scores. Value is created only when the committee chair acts on the findings.

04

Board hiring is not CXO hiring

The client meets only the candidate they are minded to appoint. We handle the search, and every ego, with care.

The board universe

The full board universe, not the independent seat alone.

01

Governance boards

Group level structures spanning multiple legal entities under one family, sometimes constituted overseas.

02

Advisory councils

Non statutory boards built for specific purposes, such as succession or a strategic question.

03

Family boards and councils

Including the appointment of independent external members to the family council.

04

Non executive directors

The non executive, non independent seat, used where a candidate is conflicted or has exhausted tenure.

05

Emeritus chairpersons

An under exploited construct that has to be structured properly, with rights and remuneration defined.

06

Those Charged With Governance

An evolving construct expected to have wide ranging impact, including pre audit engagement.
