Why this firm exists

Three gaps that personal networks cannot close.

Board work is mandated for thousands of companies, yet it remains largely unserved by any dedicated, independent institution. We were built to own the combination that no one else holds.

The composition gap

Search, not courtesy

Seats are filled through personal address books rather than structured, independent search. The annual evaluation every listed company must run is too often a compliance file, not a source of insight.

The capability gap

Both halves, in one place

Search firms treat board seats as an extension of CXO hiring. Governance consultants advise on process but hold no director talent. Neither party holds both the discipline and the network.

The ownership gap

India owned, board led

Global brands carry parent reporting lines and sanction sensitivity that governance sensitive boards find uncomfortable. An India owned platform turns that discomfort into a durable advantage.

The expanded mandate

We stake claim to the full board universe, not the independent director alone.

The seats around the table are widening well beyond the independent director. We hold the discipline and the talent for every construct a modern board now needs.

i

Governance boards

Group-level structures spanning multiple legal entities under one family, sometimes constituted overseas for a global flavour.

ii

Advisory councils

Non-statutory boards built for specific purposes, such as succession or a particular strategic question.

iii

Family boards and councils

An increasingly critical area, including the appointment of independent external members to the family council.

iv

Non-executive directors

The non-executive, non-independent seat, used where a candidate is conflicted or has exhausted tenure limits.

v

Emeritus chairpersons

An under-exploited construct that has to be structured properly, with rights, obligations and remuneration defined.

vi

Those Charged With Governance

An evolving construct expected to have wide-ranging impact, including on pre-audit-committee engagement.

How we work

Method where the decision is still made by habit.

i

Search led, not nomination led

We advocate that board composition be a disciplined, independent search rather than a personal favour, while respecting the discretion the network has always provided.

ii

A pipeline already held

We do not begin searching when the mandate lands. From a standing pool of several hundred sitting and aspiring directors, we can say within hours which two or three names fit a seat.

iii

Evaluation that is acted upon

Our framework is built to produce insight, not uniform scores. An evaluation creates value only when the committee chair acts on the findings and works with directors to lift the board.

iv

Board hiring is not CXO hiring

The client meets only the candidate they are minded to appoint. Parading several senior people is an affront to their standing, so we handle the search, and every ego, with care.
